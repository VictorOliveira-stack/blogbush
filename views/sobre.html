GB     Mondial Posting é um blog criado para compartilhar aventuras,
conhecimentos, técnicas de sobrevivência e bushcraft, além de curiosidades
sobre o mundo que conectam com nosso estilo de vida.

Mondial Posting is a blog created to share adventures, knowledge,
survival and bushcraft techniques, as well as curiosities about
the world that connect with our lifestyle.
Launched in 2024, the project is developed by Victor Oliveira,
with the purpose of inspiring and informing those who seek a deeper
connection with nature and outdoor living.
